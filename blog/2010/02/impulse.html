Impulse

2010-02-03 02:05:16
By jsliang

In Blog.

It occurs quite often these days. I feel the impulse to type or write something, though there's nothing to be said. This reminds me of my teenage days, when I insanely enjoyed writing novels or poems, pushing myself so hard to compose things I don't really feel or understand...
